Google CTF 2023 Under Construction (Web) Writeup

Writeup for under construction challenge from google ctf 2023

Introduction

This writeup is for the under construction challenge from web category in Google CTF 2023. The challenge was provided with the following statement: ‚ÄúWe were building a web app but the new CEO wants it remade in php.‚Äù The challenge contained 2 different web apps. One was in PHP that seems to be under development whilst flask app seemed like a full application. This writeup covers an Parameter Pollution vulnerability we were able to create an escalated user by supplying more than 1 parameter.

Writeup

So, at first i visited the URLS and got to know the application surface. Then i downloaded the challenge files available and opened them in the editor.

Reading the code, i got to the flag point. In the index.php file, we have:
